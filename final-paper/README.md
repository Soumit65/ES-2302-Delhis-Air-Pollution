This was my final paper for the course. It's a shared work between Arsh Verma and I. We were asked to write paper that uses data to disrupt popular narratives around Delhis Air pollution. We decided to disrupt the narrative that Delhi's pollution is just an emission by showcasing the dependence of the movement of the pollutants on the weather of Delhi at any given time.
Using time series methoda as well as statistical methods that we learnt that the pollution in delhi has a strong co relation with the weather, especially and interestingly rainfall, and not just in ways you'd think!
